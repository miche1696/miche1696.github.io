DataLight: An Energy Balancing Service

Shifting energy demand dynamically to reduce grid strain and promote sustainability.

Project Overview

DataLight shifts data center computational loads in time and space to ease grid strain by aligning energy consumption with the availability of renewable energy. By dynamically relocating computational tasks to regions where green energy is more accessible, DataLight optimizes energy use and supports grid resilience. This innovative approach to energy flexibility earned second place in the 2024 Innovation 4 Change "Energy Flexibility Challenge" by Schneider Electric.

Key Highlights:

  • Moves energy demand through data, reducing strain on power grids.
  • Enhances energy efficiency in data centers while promoting sustainability.
  • Scalable solution targeting 10% of European data centers by 2024, with plans to expand significantly by 2034.
